Software & AI Engineer
-----------------

I'm a Computer Science graduate (3.90 GPA, Highest Honors, University of Sharjah) who works across the full stack of building intelligent systems, from training and benchmarking ML models to shipping the web apps that put them in users' hands.

My research focuses on applied machine learning for healthcare. I'm first author on GlucoTwin, a digital-twin platform for early prediction of gestational diabetes (Springer EHB 2025, EDEC 2026), and a co-author on work in liver-cancer biomarker prediction and explainable AI. I care a lot about models that are accurate and interpretable.

On the engineering side, I build production-grade applications with Next.js, TypeScript, Prisma, and Flask, and deploy with Docker, AWS, and Vercel. I've shipped ML APIs, SaaS platforms, and a blockchain-powered app that won Gold at Hackmos 2024.

* 🌍  I'm based in Egypt
* ✉️  You can contact me at [abdullasayed10@gmail.com](mailto:abdullasayed10@gmail.com)
